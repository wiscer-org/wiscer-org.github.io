About wiscer.org

Wiscer.org

Wiscer is short for with screen reader.

Our Vision

Our vision is to enable people with screen readers in the area of online activities and software development.

Our Missions

  • Provide screen reader users with more guidance and learning digital materials.
  • Ensure screen reader users have access to more accessible websites and apps.
  • Increase community awareness about screen readers and accessibility.

Our Activities

  • Produce digital content to help screen reader users engage in online activities and learn software development.
  • Develop more screen reader-friendly websites/apps by participating in open source projects or creating new ones.
  • Create digital content to assist developers or organizations in developing more screen reader-friendly websites/apps.

The digital content could be dev.to articles or YouTube videos.

Screen Reader and Technologies

A screen reader is a type of computer software that reads the content on a computer screen aloud. Screen readers are used by people with vision problems (e.g., blindness, vision impairment, etc.) or by individuals who can process voice better than visual information.

We currently focus on the NVDA screen reader on Windows 10 or Windows 11. NVDA is a Windows-based free open source screen reader developed by NVAccess. NVDA is funded by grants and donations.

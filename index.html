About wiscer.org

Wiscer.og

Wiscer is a short for with screen reader.

Our vision

Our vision is to enable people with screen reader in the area of online activities and software ddevelopment.

Our Mission

  • Screen reader users to have more guidance and learning digital materials.
  • Screen reader users to have more accessible web sites and apps.
  • Community in general to have more awareness about screen reader and accessibility.

Our Activities

  • Produce digital content to ehlp screen reader users to do online activities and learn software development.
  • Develop more screen reader friendly web / apps by involve in open source projects or develop new ones.
  • Produce digital content to help developers or organizations to develop more screen reader friendly web / apps.

The digital content could be dev.to articles or Youtube videos.

Screen Reader and Technologies

Screen reader is a type of computer software that read what is on computer to voice. Screen readers are used by people with vision proble (e.g. : blindness, vision impairment, etc) or people who can process voice better than vision.

We currently focus on NVDA screen reader on Windows 10 or Windows 11. NVDA is a Windows based free open source screen reader developed by NVAccess. NVDA is funded by grants and donations.

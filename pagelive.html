Why PageLive?

Using a screen reader on Gemini means constant, manual switching between input, responses, and side panels. It slows down your workflow. PageLive eliminates the friction. It monitors the page and puts crucial actions—like navigating to the input or reviewing the last response—right at your fingertips.

Streamline Your Gemini Workflow

Action Shortcut Why You'll Use It
Jump to Input Shift + Esc Instantly start typing your next prompt.
Review Last Response Ctrl + Shift + Enter Quickly re-read the AI's last reply.
Toogle Page Info Dialog Ctrl + / Get a quick overview of the current page and access all PageLive keyboard shortcuts.
Jump to Chat List on Side Navigation Press Enter on Heading 1 in the Page Info dialog Quickly move focus to the previous chat list on side navigation. From here users can switch to other chat or access Gemini's each chat action menu, e.g.: rename, etc.
Start New Chat Ctrl + Alt + O Clear the deck and begin fresh in a single press.
Delete Current Chat Ctrl + Shift + Backspace Efficiently remove the current chat from your list. This show Gemini's default chat deletion confirmation

Content Map: Your Conversation Roadmap

The Content Map (Ctrl + M) gives you a brief, sequential summary of everything that has been asked and answered in the current chat. This helps you quickly regain context and understand the flow of a long conversation.

  • Quick Jump: Each summary entry in the dialog is a button. Pressing Enter on any prompt or response will close the map and immediately move your focus to that specific item on the main page.
